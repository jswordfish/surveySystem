Business Perspective

I understand how my goals and KPIs fit with our broader business strategy.

I know how the various functions at Colgate contribute to achieve Colgate’s strategic priorities.

I understand how actions by my function can have a positive or negative impact on other functions at Colgate.

I have a clear understanding of how Colgate delivers shareholder value, customer value and employee value.

I understand the competitive dynamics faced by Colgate in today’s market environment.

Problem Solving

Once a problem (or opportunity) is identified I am able to clearly define the problem and goal statement.

I am able to analyze the problem using multiple problem analysis tools to clearly understand the situation.

I am able to generate multiple solutions to solve the problem.

I am able to choose the best possible solution to resolve the problem.

I am able to implement a solution and ensure the goal statement is obtained.

Communication Skills

I am able to structure my message in a clear and concise manner.

I project confidence when communicating through my voice.

I project confidence when communicating through my body language.

I am able to adapt my message based on the person and the situation.

Influencing Skills

I feel confident when influencing others to adopt my recommendation, initiative or ideas.

I am able to adapt my influencing approach based on my stakeholders and the situation.

I am able to take a strategic approach in terms of planning and communicating with my stakeholders when influencing.

I am able to effective utilize the knowledge, skills and experience of others when influencing my stakeholders.

I am able to follow-up with my stakeholders to ensure my ideas, recommendations and initiatives are implemented.

Self-Awareness

I have a clear understanding of my leadership strengths.

I have a clear understanding of my development areas.

I am aware of my leadership and communication style and the impact it has on others in the organization.

I understand how I want to be seen by others in the organization.

I am able to adapt my words, voice, body language and actions to achieve my desired impression.
