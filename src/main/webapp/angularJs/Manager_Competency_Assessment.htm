Business Perspective

Understands how his/her goals and KPIs fit with our broader business strategy.

Knows how the various functions at Colgate contribute to achieve Colgate’s strategic priorities.

Has a clear understanding of how Colgate delivers shareholder value, customer value and employee value.

Understands how actions by his/her function can have a positive or negative impact on other functions at Colgate.

Understands the competitive dynamics faced by Colgate in today’s market environment.

Problem Solving

Once a problem (or opportunity) is identified he/she is able to clearly define the problem and goal statement.

Can analyze the problem using multiple problem analysis tools to clearly understand the situation.

Can generate multiple solutions to solve the problem.

Can choose the best possible solution to resolve the problem.

Can implement a solution and ensure the goal statement is obtained.

Communication Skills

Can structure his/her message in a clear and concise manner.

Projects confidence when communicating through his/her voice.

Projects t confidence when communicating through his/her body language.

Can adapt his/her message based on the person and the situation.

Influencing Skills

Is confident when influencing others to adopt his/her recommendation, initiative or ideas.

Can adapt his/her influencing approach based on his/her stakeholders and the situation.

Can take a strategic approach in terms of planning and communicating with his/her stakeholders when influencing.

Can effective utilize the knowledge, skills and experience of others when influencing his/her stakeholders.

Can follow-up with my stakeholders to ensure my ideas, recommendations and initiatives are implemented.

Self-Awareness

Has a clear understanding of his/her leadership strengths.

Has a clear understanding of his/her development areas.

Is aware of his/her leadership and communication style and the impact it has on others in the organization.

Understands how he/she want to be seen by others in the organization.

Is able to adapt his/her words, voice, body language and actions to achieve his/her desired impression.
